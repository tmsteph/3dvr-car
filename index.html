3dvr-car

An open-source electric vehicle by 3dvr.tech

🚙 What is 3dvr-car?

The 3dvr-car is a modular, sustainable, and repairable electric vehicle. Designed for creators, off-grid workers, and digital nomads, it's more than a car — it’s your mobile workspace, kinetic power hub, and freedom machine.

🌱 Vision

  • Built with sustainable, repairable materials (like hemp composites)
  • Electric-powered with solar and kinetic charging options
  • Modular hardware and software for full customization
  • Open blueprints and community-driven development
